I don’t quite know where to begin with this. If you aren’t shaking your head vigorously by the end of the article there’s something wrong with you.

“High On Hashtags” is described in the festival program thus: “Dazed and confused, consumers and brands alike are trippin’ on hashtags,” it read. They’re tripping on something, all right. “Who here lost a bet, and that’s why you’re here?” asked one of the presenters, Eric Bader. After that, he and his co-host said the word “hashtag” for an hour, pausing to take occasional questions.
