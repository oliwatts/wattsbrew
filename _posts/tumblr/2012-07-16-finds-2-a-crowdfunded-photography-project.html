Speaking of crowdsourced funding my brother Harry is launching a new photography project, and he’s looking for donations.

The original issue of FINDS was distributed guerilla-style during the Brighton Fringe Festival 2010 and around various locations in London, creating a social frenzy to ‘find’ FINDS.
FINDS was a great project, leaving newspapers at random places containing rather cool photos of found and discarded material. FINDS 2 will do the same across Brighton and London.

£10 will get you a copy of both the original FINDS and the new project, and £60 will get you copies of all of Harry’s work.

Harry is a brilliant photographer, regularly shoots for the Telegraph, has worked with Salvatore Ferragamo and photographers Martin Parr and Ewen Spencer.

In addition to being a brilliant photographer, he’s also one of the finest men on the planet.
